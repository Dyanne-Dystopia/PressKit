Dyanne Dystopia

They/She

My name is Dyanne Dystopia, DyDy (Dee Dee) for short. I am a Drag Performer from Juneau, Alaska that has been performing for the past two years. My drag is heavily inspired by 1920's glamour with a focus on electro swing and freedom of movement. I find the female liberation movement that began in the 1920's with the flapper girl to be a fabulous inspiration for my drag. My performances convey freedom and authenticity through the medium of burlesque and movement. I have competed nationally at Miss Gay America 2025 attending as the first alternate for Miss Gay Alaska America 2025, I will be competing for Miss Gay Alaska America 2027 as I am driven to snatch the crown. My drag career began in 2023 when I was unwittingly cast in a show to cover for a sick drag king. I caught the bug and have been performing on a regular basis since. As a way to break into the main drag shows in my city, shortly after starting my drag career, I competed in Miss Gay America 2024 placing as the second alternate. Following my exploits in the Miss Gay Alaska pageant I was brought on as a cast member with Juneau Drag run by the fabulous Gigi Moneroe. I have been performing with Juneau Drag ever since. This year I established my own company, House of Dystopia LLC, and I am working on expanding from the Juneau market. Juneau will always be home but I am driven to take advantage of every opportunity I can to continue to grow in my art. I am an extremely driven queen that values punctuality, preparedness, and creativity.
